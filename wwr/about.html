Purpose

Our purpose is to share the thrill of Mukungwa River white water rafting. Our mission is to provide safe, unforgettable adventures. We believe in excitement, natural beauty, and lasting memories. Come, raft with us!

History

Founded in 2023, A Thousand Hills White Water Rafting Company emerged from a deep passion for Rwanda's natural beauty and the thrill of river adventures.

Whether you're a first-time paddler or a seasoned thrill-seeker, we offer guided trips tailored to every skill level, ensuring both excitement and.
