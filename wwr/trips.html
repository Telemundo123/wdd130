Featured Trips

Mukungwa River Express (Half-Day)

Experience the exhilarating rapids of the Mukungwa River on this action-packed half-day trip. Perfect for those looking for a quick dose of adrenaline!

Kivu Lake Serenity (Full-Day)

A full day of adventure, starting with the Mukungwa rapids and transitioning to the calmer, scenic waters leading into Lake Kivu. Enjoy stunning views and a delicious riverside lunch.

Gentle Mukungwa Float (Half-Day)

Perfect for families and first-timers, this relaxing half-day float on the calmer sections of the Mukungwa River offers gentle currents, scenic views, and an enjoyable introduction to rafting without the big rapids.

Rwanda Rafting Expedition (2-Day)

For the ultimate adventure, join our two-day expedition! Explore more of the Mukungwa River, camp under the stars, and truly immerse yourself in Rwanda's natural beauty.
